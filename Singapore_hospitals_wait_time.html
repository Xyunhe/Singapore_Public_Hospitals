About A&E Services in Singapore

Accident & Emergency (A&E) departments in Singapore public hospitals provide 24-hour emergency care for patients with serious injuries or illnesses.

When to visit A&E

  • Severe chest pain or difficulty breathing
  • Severe bleeding that cannot be stopped
  • Severe burns or scalds
  • Suspected stroke or heart attack
  • Serious head injuries
  • Loss of consciousness

Alternatives for non-emergencies

  • Visit a GP clinic for non-urgent medical advice
  • Visit a 24-hour clinic for after-hours care
  • Call 995 for medical emergencies requiring an ambulance
  • Call 1777 for non-emergency ambulance services
  • Use telemedicine services for minor conditions
